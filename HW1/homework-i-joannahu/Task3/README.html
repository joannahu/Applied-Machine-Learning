Function (mean,std) meanstd(X,axis) computes mean and standard deviation of input data.

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}mean = \frac{\sum x_i }{N}\\std = \sqrt{\frac{(\sum x_i - mean)^2}{N}}\\\begin{split}Let X = \begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{array}\end{split}\\if axis = 0, mean = \begin{array}{c}\\\begin{split}3 \\\end{split}\\\begin{split}7.5 \\\end{split}\\12 \end{array}\\if axis = 1, mean = \begin{array}{ccc}\\6 & 7.5 & 9 \end{array}\\if axis = NULL, mean = 45\end{aligned}\end{align} \]
